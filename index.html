Pre-registered methodology experiment

Does a Quality-by-Design protocol body actually outperform a traditional one under ICH E6(R3)?

Two arms of the same synthetic GC-301 Phase 2b psoriasis study, stress-tested against 99 protocol-change scenarios drawn from EMA GCP IWG 2024, FDA BIMO FY17–FY24, and the post-2024 RBM / DCT / amendment-driver literature. Arm A follows current industry-traditional drafting. Arm B operationalizes E6(R3)'s Critical-to-Quality factor register inside the protocol body.
